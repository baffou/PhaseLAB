    .. code-block:: matlab
        :caption: Prototypes

        % possible forms
        Objective(Mobj)
        Objective(Mobj, NA)
        Objective(Mobj, NA, obj_brand)

        % examples
        Objective(100)
        Objective(100, 1.3)
        Objective(100, 1.3, 'Olympus')

    .. raw:: html
        
        <div class="title">Description</div>

    ``Mobj`` is the magnification written of the objective, ``NA`` is the numerical aperture of the objective, ``obj_brand`` is the brand of the objective.
